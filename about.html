Our Story

ہماری کہانی

For over 26 years, Akram Fish House & Restaurant has been a beacon of authentic Pakistani cuisine. What started as a humble fish fry stall has grown into one of the most trusted names in Desi and Fish cuisine.

Founded by Akram Khan and Asghar Khan, our restaurant has built its reputation on the freshest ingredients, traditional recipes passed down through generations, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Our chefs specialize in fresh fish preparations — from crispy Fish Fry to smoky Bar B Q, creamy Handi, and flavorful Salan. We also serve the finest Mutton and Chicken Karahi, Chinese cuisine, BBQ, and a complete range of Desi delicacies.
